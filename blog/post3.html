My Science Experiment.

As you can see me picking up the specimen, science can be very dangerous. At that time I didn't really know what it was and I was just curious, so I researched it myself. But the thing is, I wasn't prepared at all for that little jump on my face. It was terrifying to say the least.

However, this was all preventable if I had the proper procedures in handling this specimen. There was no cover to the plate, and I wasn't wearing any special protection in case something happened. I also should have someone with me or have some type of way to contact someone if this ever happened. Lesson learned, have safety gear.

Anyways, I am lucky to survive, and I'll take this chance to instill some safety procedures and gear for our lab. I will also have to report to the higher ups, about the danger of this specimen, and if we could do further testing if possible.
