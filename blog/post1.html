My Morning Routine.

My morning routine is not that special. I wake up roughly around 3:15AM and leave my house at 3:30AM. Then I hitch a ride with my dad when he goes to work at 5AM. Afterwards, I take the car or the bus to go to Scott Road Park and Ride, if I have the car, or I go straight to Surrey Central, if I take the bus. I try to arrive on campus around 7:30-7:40AM so I have time to revise, review, or work on some assignments for a class. But before I go inside the Campus, I actually order myself some coffee from the nearby Tim Hortons, so I can be energized and it helps keep me awake.

That is roughly my morning routine, since after having classes, I just find a way home, but that's a story to be told some other day.

Also to note, my morning routine during weekdays and weekends are somewhat different. However, the only difference is that during weekends, I get to sleep a few hours in and I can drink as much coffee as I want.
