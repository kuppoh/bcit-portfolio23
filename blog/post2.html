My Fantasy Adventures.

Unfortunately, most of my fantasy adventures are through imagination, however, I must admit that I always had thoughts of wanting to experience something like it. I always have the weirdest, craziest, and most adventurous imaginations, so much so, that I even started to write some of these things down to better remember them.

The reason why I have such imaginations are due to various influences. I have watched the LOTR Trilogy so many times, its a wonder how I have not yet to become tired of watching them. You can definitely say, I am a LOTR fanatic. Another reason is D&D, it may sound nerdy, but the endless possiblities that you can create and imagine is truly fantastic.

Lately, however, I am experiencing less and less of such imaginations. Most likely due to the fact that I have resumed my schooling. To get around that, I found watching D&D roleplaying channels, like Dimension20 or Critical Role. I even resorted to watching some fantasy movies and series, and you can bet I watched LOTR many times. Anyways, its always good to take a step out of reality and relax for a bit, just to ease our minds off from any stressors and have a little time for ourselves, besides, it doesn't hurt to do so.
